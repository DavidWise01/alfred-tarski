# ALFRED TARSKI · XIX

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **LOGIKĒ**.

ALFRED TARSKI (1901–1983) — LOGIKĒ XIX. 'True' is logic's most-used word, and before Tarski no one had said precisely what it MEANS without circularity or paradox. In 1933 he pinned it down: ⚑ SEMANTIC TRUTH — a sentence is true exactly when it FITS the structure it describes; 'snow is white' is true IFF snow is white (Convention T); a formula's truth is always RELATIVE TO A MODEL. This founded MODEL THEORY (half of modern logic; the semantics under database queries, formal verification, program correctness). ⚑ THE UNDEFINABILITY THEOREM (a semantic sibling of [[kurt-godel]]): no sufficiently rich language can define its OWN truth predicate → 'true' must live in a higher METALANGUAGE → which is exactly why the LIAR paradox dissolves (stratify object-language vs metalanguage and the self-referential sentence can't be formed — same cure as [[bertrand-russell]]'s types). LIVE: (1) truth-in-a-model — toggle atoms p,q,r, evaluate φ=(p∧q)→r, and see the SAME sentence true in one preset model and false in another (verified M₁ false, M₂ true); (2) the Liar — both branches spin forever in one language, resolved by the object/metalanguage split. CONFIDENCE-TIERED: STRONG (semantic truth & model theory — rigorous, foundational, the basis of logical consequence & computing semantics) / TRUTH-IS-LANGUAGE-RELATIVE (⚠ defined for FORMAL languages, relative to a model & metalanguage — a precise mathematical relation, NOT a theory of Truth-about-reality) / THE-'PARADOX'-ISN'T-PHYSICS (⚠ Banach–Tarski: a ball → two balls is a real theorem about IDEALIZED point-sets needing the Axiom of Choice & non-measurable pieces with no volume — says nothing about cutting real matter; pop 'duplication machine' accounts are wrong). The man: a Polish Jew lecturing in the USA in Sept 1939 when Germany invaded — the trip saved his life though much of his family was murdered in the Holocaust; became the great founder of logic at Berkeley. ⚑ With Tarski the foundations rank rests: from [[thales-of-miletus]]' first proof through the gate, the limits, and the meaning of truth itself.
